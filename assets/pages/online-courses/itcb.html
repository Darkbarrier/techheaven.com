Introduction to Computer Basics

Course Overview

Introduction to Computer Basics course is designed for beginners looking to build their foundational computer skills. Whether you're new to using computers or looking to sharpen your skills, this course provides an easy and practical approach to learning.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic computer operations
  • Introduction to MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Managing files and folders
  • Basic internet usage (browsing, email, etc.)
  • Understanding cloud storage
  • Creating and editing documents

Course Highlights

  • Hands-on training with real-world examples
  • Interactive tutorials for better understanding
  • Guidance from experienced instructors
  • Accessible online learning

By the end of this course, you'll have the confidence to handle basic computer tasks with ease and efficiency, making you more productive in both your personal and professional life.
